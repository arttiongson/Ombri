About Ombri

Most finance apps treat money like an emergency. Ombri doesn’t.

The interface takes on the color of the hour and the weather outside your window — morning warmth, afternoon clarity, evening quiet, a different quality of light when it rains. Every number is still there. It just stops yelling.

What Ombri does

  • Plugs into your bank.

    Secure connection through Plaid. Accounts, cards, and investments arrive already categorized.

  • Finds your bills.

    Recurring charges surface themselves — rent, subscriptions, utilities, anything with a pattern.

  • Tracks every payment.

    Every transaction, searchable by amount, merchant, category, or date.

  • Tells you before you overspend.

    A gentle notification when a category’s trending hot. Never an alarm.

Four times of day

Morning, afternoon, sunset, night. The home screen re-renders itself as the sun moves.
